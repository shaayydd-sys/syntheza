Our Story
Forged in Hong Kong,
Scaled Globally.

Incorporated in February 2026 in Hong Kong SAR, Sinteza Trading Limited was established to bridge the expanding gap between prime chemical producers and the rapidly accelerating markets of Asia, Africa, and Europe.

We are not brokers. We act as principals in every transaction, deploying capital to secure allocations, manage freight risk, and guarantee delivery under the strictest timelines.
